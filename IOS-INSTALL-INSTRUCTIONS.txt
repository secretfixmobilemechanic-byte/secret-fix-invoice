SECRET FIX INVOICE MAKER — IPHONE INSTALLATION
================================================

This app is free and does not require a subscription. Invoice data stays in
Safari storage on the iPhone unless you share or email it.

IMPORTANT: Apple requires a PWA to be opened from a secure website before it
can be installed. Opening index.html directly from the Files app will show the
invoice maker, but iOS will not install it or enable its offline cache.

INSTALL FROM SAFARI
-------------------
1. Upload the complete Secret-Fix-Invoice-PWA folder to any HTTPS static host.
   Free choices include GitHub Pages, Cloudflare Pages, or Netlify Drop.
2. Open the resulting HTTPS address in Safari on the iPhone.
3. Wait for the full invoice page to load once.
4. Tap Safari's Share button (the square with the upward arrow).
5. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen," then tap "Add."
6. Open Secret Fix from the new Home Screen icon.
7. To verify offline use, open it once, turn on Airplane Mode, and reopen it.

SAVING AN INVOICE
-----------------
- Changes are saved automatically on that iPhone after you edit a field.
- Tap "Save Draft" for an immediate confirmation.
- The current draft returns when the app is reopened.
- Photos selected from the camera are not kept after the app closes; this is an
  iPhone privacy restriction. The invoice fields and signatures are retained.
- Tap "Clear" only when you want to erase the saved draft and start over.

SAVE OR PRINT A PDF OFFLINE ON IPHONE
-------------------------------------
1. Open the invoice and tap "Export PDF."
2. On the iOS print screen, place two fingers on the invoice preview and spread
   them apart (pinch outward). This opens the preview as a PDF.
3. Tap Share.
4. Choose "Save to Files" to keep the PDF, "Print" to print it, or choose an app
   such as Mail/Gmail to send it.

The PDF/print flow works offline. Sending through Gmail requires an internet
connection. If "Send via Gmail" cannot prepare an attachment while offline,
use Export PDF first and attach the saved PDF after reconnecting.

BACKUP NOTE
-----------
Safari can erase website data if the app is removed or browser data is cleared.
Save completed invoices as PDFs in Files for permanent records.
